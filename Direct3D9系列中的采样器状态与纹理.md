# Direct3D9系列中的采样器状态与纹理  

* **纹理寻址方式**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_ADDRESSU`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_ADDRESSV`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_ADDRESSW`**  
* **纹理插值方式**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_BORDERCOLOR`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MAGFILTER`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MINFILTER`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MIPFILTER`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MAXMIPLEVEL`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MIPMAPLODBIAS`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_MAXANISOTROPY`**  
* **杂项**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_SRGBTEXTURE`**  
    * **`D3DSAMP_ELEMENTINDEX`**  
* _置换贴图（固定渲染管线，不要使用）_  
    * _`D3DSAMP_DMAPOFFSET` (不要使用)_
